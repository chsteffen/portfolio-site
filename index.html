Why did you become a web developer and user experience designer?

I enjoy the challenges that come with web development and user experience design. Web development asks me to create something from nothing, and user experience design helps me remember the purpose of that creation. Both fields of expertise allow me to continue to learn and collaborate with others.

Who are your clients?

My clients are small businesses, conservation efforts and startups.

What is your project turnaround?

I will generally take two weeks to see how far I can get on the website design or redesign and then check in with my client with a status update. This check in allows the client to tell me what needs to be changed, and it gives me the opportunity to discuss the next steps and what I need to proceed with the project.
